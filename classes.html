Padel at
Semper Fit

The fastest-growing racquet sport in the world, now in Nairobi. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned player, our professional courts, certified coaches, and vibrant padel community are ready for you.

01

Padel Courts

Two fully-enclosed, glass-walled padel courts to international standards. Excellent court surface, consistent bounce, and proper lighting for day and evening play.

02

Expert Coaching

Our certified padel coaches work with beginners and competitive players alike. Learn the rules, master technique, and develop your game from the ground up, or sharpen elite skills.

03

Easy Court Booking

Book a court by the hour, 7 days a week from 5am-8am & 4-8pm (Mon-Fri). Flexible slots for casual play, coaching sessions, or corporate events. Rackets and balls available for hire.

04

Leagues & Tournaments

Compete in our internal padel leagues and tournaments. From friendly mixers to serious ranked play, we create competitive opportunities that keep you improving and engaged.
