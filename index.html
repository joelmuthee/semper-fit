About Semper Fit

More Than a Gym.
A Community.

Semper Fit Gymnasium is where commitment meets excellence. Located at SCIS, we offer a world-class training environment designed for every fitness level, from beginners finding their stride to elite athletes pushing their limits.

Our philosophy is simple: consistent effort produces extraordinary results. Whether you're here for the gym floor, a boxing session, or a fierce padel match, Semper Fit is your arena.

Nairobi's Premier Padel

Play Padel.
Love the Game.

Padel is the world's fastest-growing racket sport, and Semper Fit is leading the charge in Nairobi. Our professional courts offer the perfect arena for beginners and competitive players alike.

What We Offer

World-Class Facilities

Everything you need for a complete fitness lifestyle, all in one place.

Strength Training

A fully equipped free weights zone with dumbbells, barbells, squat racks, and cable machines for every strength goal.

Cardio Zone
