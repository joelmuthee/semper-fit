Who We Are

More Than a Gym.
A Community.

Semper Fit Gymnasium was founded with a singular belief: that everyone deserves access to world-class fitness facilities and expert guidance. Nestled at SCIS in Nairobi, we've built a space where athletes, beginners, and everyone in between come to challenge themselves and grow.

The name "Semper Fit", always fit, reflects our core philosophy. Fitness isn't a destination; it's a lifelong commitment. We're here to make that journey as rewarding, effective, and enjoyable as possible.

From state-of-the-art strength equipment to professional padel courts, every corner of our facility has been designed to elevate your performance and keep you coming back.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional fitness experience that empowers every member to achieve their personal best, through world-class facilities, expert coaching, and an unbeatable community spirit.

Our Vision

To be Nairobi's most trusted fitness destination, a place where the pursuit of health, strength, and sport brings people together and transforms lives, one session at a time.
